Our Studio Model

Three flexible partnership approaches designed to meet you where you are in your venture journey.

Tech-for-Equity

We build your MVP and product roadmap in exchange for equity stake in your venture.

  • Full technical team
  • Product strategy
  • Go-to-market support
  • Ongoing mentorship

Cash-Funded

Traditional development partnership with fixed payments and deliverables.

  • Fixed-price projects
  • Agile development
  • Quality assurance
  • Post-launch support

Hybrid

Combination of cash and equity for large-scale ventures with high growth potential.

  • Reduced cash investment
  • Long-term partnership
  • Shared risk & reward
  • Strategic guidance
